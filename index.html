What is an active client?
An active client is any patient or client you have not archived. In short, you determine what an active client is. You can archive and unarchive as you please. Archived clients are not counted toward your limit and you cannot bill or add notes to an archived client.
Do you charge per therapist or staff member?
Unlike most therapy notes software, we do not charge per staff/therapist, no matter if you have 1 therapist or 200 — so you can use your account with as many people as you’d like and never have to worry about extra charges.
I have more than 150 active clients, will TheraNest work for me?
Definitely yes. We simply want you to contact us if you have more than 150 active clients so we can provide the kind of resource you'll need when you subscribe.
What is the final cost to file electronic claims?

What other costs are associated with using TheraNest?
There are no hidden costs to using TheraNest. However, there are optional services provided that you may choose to use such as our automated reminder service, insurance claims service, credit card processing, and data migration services.

The cost of our reminder services is 5 cents/text and 15 cents per call (U.S. & Canada rates). Email is free. Contact us to discuss data migration costs. They are based on volume and type of data you are migrating.
What happens after my 30 day trial?
You can upgrade to any subscription any time during your trial. However, if you hit the end of your trial without choosing a subscription plan, your account will be put on hold (your files and data will all stay safe and sound, don't worry) until you choose a subscription (or cancel).
Do I have to sign a long term contract?
No. There’s no contracts on monthly plans — you just pay as you go, and you can cancel any time without penalty. You can also switch between plans at anytime, and you'll be prorated for time spent on each plan during the month.
